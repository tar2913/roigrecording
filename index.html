Taylor Roig has worked as a professional audio engineer for over 12 years, earning a reputation high quality, dedicated, and friendly work, both live and in the studio.

Taylor is a regular engineer at Jazz at Lincoln Center. He has worked at Electric Lady as well as recording at countless studios and live venues in the NYC area. 

A rigorous self-teacher, Taylor also studied at the SAE School from 2007 - 2008, giving him a unique mix of studied perfectionism and innovative approaches. He has received the 2014 Alumni Award for Outstanding Achievement in Live Sound.

Taylor's enthusiasm for music and the craft of audio engineering is matched only by his skill at achieving an artist's vision.
